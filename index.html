    background

    Previously I worked in Gothenburg for companies such as Volvo GTT, RapidImages, and won competitions for Spotify and Volvo GTT. But perhaps my most notable role was as a consultant for Consid. There, I worked on assignments for government agencies like the City of Gothenburg and the Swedish Agency for Marine and Water Management.

    One of my greatest achievements was is my work at the Swedish Agency for Marine and Water Management, where I redesigned and created a Python plugin for retrieving satellite images in QGIS.
    My design increased the productivity of their GIS developers by reducing the time it takes to find the right satellite data by up to 95%. From several minutes of searching to a few seconds of overview and clicks. It also saved them and taxpayers up to 1 600 000 SEK per year in license costs for their developer teams. Beyond the company it also cut a potential cost for those 70 000 people who downloaded the open-source plugin online.

    Currently, I'm a self-employed freelancer in both web development and design. I am looking for working on a more permanent team, and am open to a full-time position at the right company.
